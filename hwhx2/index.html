The Slice

  • The "multitool" board
  • RP2040
  • 12 GPIOs
  • 2 LEDs
  • BOOTSEL and! RESET
  • Preloaded μPython
  • Do whatever you want with it
  • POWER is GND || +3.3

The Pizza

  • The target board
  • SEcube by Blu5
  • STM32F4 - ARM Cortex-M4
  • MachXO2-7000 FPGA (6864 LUTs)
  • USB-C for power only
  • JTAG for upload only
  • 20 GPIOs for challenge interface
